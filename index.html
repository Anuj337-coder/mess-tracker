Platform Features

Everything You Need to Fight Food Waste

📈

Food Waste Analytics

Interactive weekly bar charts showing waste trends, cooked vs wasted comparison, and improvement highlights.

🏥

NGO Directory

Browse verified NGO profiles with location, accepted food types, contact info, and star ratings.

🧮

Smart Quantity Estimator

Enter guest count and event type — get instant food quantity suggestions (roti, rice, sabzi, desserts).

📋

Donation History

Complete log of all past donations with receipt codes, NGO names, status, and timestamps.

📧

Email Notifications

Automatic email alerts when NGO accepts or rejects your donation request — no manual follow-up needed.

🔐

Secure Authentication

JWT-based login with role-specific dashboards. Your data is protected and private.
